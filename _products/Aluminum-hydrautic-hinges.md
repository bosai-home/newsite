Product Description:
Elevate your door hardware with our premium hydraulic hinge, expertly crafted from space-grade aluminum and stainless steel for exceptional durability and corrosion resistance. Measuring 5 inches (138mm) and weighing 405g, this hinge offers a perfect balance of strength and lightweight design. Featuring advanced hydraulic buffering, this hinge allows adjustable closing force and speed, delivering smooth, controlled door operation.
