This lock cylinder is engineered for advanced residential and commercial security applications. Constructed with full brass components including the shell, lock core, and keys, it offers excellent corrosion resistance, long-term durability, and a refined, high-grade appearance. The brushed and polished surface ensures long-lasting beauty while resisting wear and oxidation.   


## Key Features:  
1.	Full Brass Structure  
Shell, plug, and blade components are all made of brass, delivering superior stability, smooth rotation, and long operating life in various climates and environments.  
2.	Super C-Level 4D-8 Slot Advanced Lock Core  
The lock adopts a high-security 4-dimensional, 8-slot core design with all-brass leaf wafers, significantly increasing complexity and anti-picking performance.  
3.	High Complexity Key System  
Featuring 144 precise tooth points combined with dual-side edge-column locking technology, the lock supports up to 20 million unique key combinations, making unauthorized key duplication extremely difficult.  
4.	Eight Solid One-Piece Metal Keys  
Comes with 8 heavy-duty, wear-resistant keys. Each key is double-milled for enhanced anti-theft performance and long service life, with no plastic joints to crack or deform.  
5.	Manganese Steel Anti-Rust Wafers  
Internal wafers are constructed from hardened manganese steel, offering 360° smooth rotation and excellent resistance against impact, hammering, and forced attacks.  
6.	Anti-Pry, Anti-Drill, and Anti-Force Design  
Reinforced anti-pry beam and closed keyway structure effectively prevent wire hooks, pin tools, and drill bits from breaking in.  
7.	Dual-Sided Security Structure  
Both ends of the cylinder are technical-security ends. When keys are inserted into both sides simultaneously, the first inserted key will have opening priority, preventing unauthorized manipulation.  
8.	Smooth Rotation and Anti-Jamming  
Precision-machined components ensure friction-free operation, providing a stable, quiet, and smooth unlocking experience.  
9.	Corrosion & Wear Resistant Surface  
Brushed polishing process enhances appearance and prevents oxidation, suitable for high-end door hardware systems.  
10.	Complies with Security Standards  
Engineered to resist technical opening, decoding attempts, and brute force. Ideal for high-security residential, office, hotel, and commercial door systems.
