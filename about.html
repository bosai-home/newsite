About TechShop

Reliable Door Hardware Manufacturer | OEM & ODM Supported | Serving Global B2B Clients

TechShop is a professional manufacturer specializing in door hardware products, including door locks, door handles, lock bodies, cylinders, and related accessories.

Founded in 2015, we operate with a strong focus on product quality, stable supply, and long-term cooperation with wholesalers, distributors, and project contractors worldwide. Our experienced R&D team and modern production facilities enable us to provide reliable OEM & ODM solutions tailored to different markets.

From raw material selection to surface treatment and final inspection, every process is strictly controlled to ensure durability, consistency, and professional finishing. Our products are exported to Europe, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and other international markets.

Partner with TechShop for stable quality, competitive factory pricing, and long-term business growth.
